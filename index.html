Forge — Privacy Policy

Build habits that stick. One day at a time.

Last updated: May 2026

Forge is built on one principle: your data is yours. We collect the minimum information needed to make the app work and help us build a better product. We don't sell your data. We don't show you ads. We don't share your information with third parties.

This policy explains exactly what we collect, why we collect it, and where it goes.

📦 What We Collect

Forge collects two categories of data: information that stays on your device, and anonymous analytics sent to help us understand how people use the app.

🔒 What We Never Collect

  • ✕ Your email address or phone number
  • ✕ Your location or GPS data
  • ✕ Your contacts or address book
  • ✕ Your full camera roll — photos are only accessed when you explicitly choose one
  • ✕ Anything sold to advertisers or third parties
  • ✕ Any data that could identify you personally

📡 Analytics

We send anonymous, aggregate analytics to CloudKit — Apple's own cloud infrastructure — to understand how people use Forge. This helps us answer questions like: which habits are most popular, at what point do people tend to stop, and do certain demographics have higher completion rates.

Every analytics event is tagged with a randomly-generated anonymous ID (a UUID stored in your device's secure Keychain). This ID has no connection to your name, email, or identity. It simply lets us understand user journeys without knowing whose journey it is.

Analytics are stored in CloudKit's public database — Apple's infrastructure, subject to Apple's privacy standards. We never run our own server. Your data never passes through third-party analytics platforms like Firebase, Mixpanel, or Amplitude.

☁️ iCloud Sync

Forge uses CloudKit to sync your habit data across your devices and ensure it survives if you reinstall the app. Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account — we cannot access it. Apple's iCloud privacy policy applies.

iCloud sync is tied to your Apple ID. If you sign out of iCloud, your data remains on your device but will no longer sync.

🧒 Children's Privacy

Forge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

Users who indicate they are under 18 during optional demographic collection are treated with additional care — their data is excluded from any aggregate reporting.

🗑️ Deleting Your Data

You can delete all your data at any time from within the app: Settings → Reset Everything. This removes all goals, check-ins, and profile information from your device and from iCloud.

Anonymous analytics events that have already been sent cannot be recalled — they contain no identifying information and cannot be linked back to you.

📋 Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data including the right to access, correct, or delete it. Since Forge stores almost everything locally on your device, you already have direct control over most of your data.

For any data-related requests, contact us at the address below and we'll respond within 30 days.
